WHO AM I?

My name is Matt, I love fitness and I want to help everyone get as fit as possible in the most sustainable way.

EARLY FITNESS

I have always been interested in fitness since I was young and have always been fairly fit. I first started working out when I was about 16 years old with a set of dumbells that I bought with my pocket money.
In my younger years I would play every sport I could but I spent most of my time playing football (soccer).

TEENS AND TWENTIES

When I started working at the age of 18 I was able to pay for a gym membership at the local gym. This was when I started taking fitness a little more serious. My workouts consisted mostly of free weights and I was a big believer in lifting as heavy as possible, I rarely spent longer than 45 minutes in the gym.
In this time I made reasonable progress, I could bench press 100kg unassisted and my weight went from 70kg to almost 80kg. This did not come without a cost though as I suffered from back pain and tight muscles.
I spent a lot of time reading about fitness and it became an obsession to some extent.

BUILT BY BODY-WEIGHT

After spending countless hours in the gym and spending over $100 per week on a gym membership I became disillusioned and was no longer enjoying fitness like I used to. The amount of misinformation and lies within the fitness industry is staggering so I decided to go it alone and do my own thing.
At this time I came across the likes of Frank Medrano and I became immediately hooked on calisthenics. The strength developed performing these types of workouts seemed much more functional and rather than endlessly trying to bench press more weight I could focus on performing different skills.

WHY TRUST ME?

I really am your average joe, I work as an engineer in Sydney, Australia but am originally from the UK. I do a lot of my workouts with my boss on our lunchbreak in the numerous outdoor gyms around Sydney. I truly believe that bodyweight workouts are the answer to not only keeping fit but also to obtaining the body you desire. I transformed my body whilst living a normal life and without spending hours in gym every day, if I can do it anybody can. If you're sick of the lies and dishonestly within the fitness industry then please give me a chance to guide you on your journey.
